About Us

The Fermentary is a Colorado artisanal food and beverage company. They strive to source ingredients from local farmers and craft a variety of unique and delicious products. The company was founded in 2020 during the global pandemic that left many of us twiddling our thumbs in the midst of our own abodes.

The Founders, Anthony & Emili, have an in-depth background in the Beverage Industry. Wine is their passion, but the Colorado craft beer s cene has ignited an interest in home brewing beer. Anthony comes from a strong Italian family that has preserved the value of home cooking. Much of that knowledge has inspired our fermented sauces and marinades. Emili has always had interest in the science behind fermentation, so she spearheads the experiments while Anthony tracks the progress.

The art and science behind proper fermentation has been studied since the beginning of humans storing food. The Fermentary intends to bring new inventive products yet relish in the working traditions from our history. We work within our community to create the best products for our consumers.
